About

I have done my MS in Computer Science from San Francisco State University. After completing my Masters, I started working in a started in bay area. Name of the startup was SugarCRM. I worked there as a fullstack developer in LAMP stack. I was involved in building various features, one of the features that I am proud of building is 'User Dashlet' for Sugar6 that consists of dashlets of the most frequently used modules by the user. I implemented it using HTML5, CSS, jQuery in the front end and PHP and MySQL in the backend. For Sugar 7.0 I have written 'Contacts' widget in javascript and related RestfulAPI in the backend. At SugarCRM, I was also involved critical (high priority) and some very complex bug fixes (involving code changes in some of the very complex modules like workflow and studio). I would also monitor daily build on jenkins. Have also written php unit tests.

After working at SugarCRM for 3 years, I realized that I needed to take a break for my family since I had my second kid then. Hence in late 2013 I left my job at Sugar and we moved to east coast since we have family here so wanted to be close to the family.

After a break of one and half years in 2015, I again started working in a small startup named codex technologies. Codex technologies works on projects for contract. There I worked remotely for a hospital website. I worked in javascript and react. I worked there for a year.

After working for quite sometime at codex I gained immense interest in react hence to gain expertise in it I applied to and got accpeted by one of the best coding boot camps of New York City, named Fullstack Academy. There I did an immersive web development course in javascript, react, redux, node, express and sequelize for 5 months. In those five months I created several applications using these technologies. One of the projects that I am extremely proud of is Codehuddle. Codehuddle is listed in my portfolio.

After completing my course at Fullstack Academy in Feb 2017, I worked as a contractor for two months at a startup known as CuratedCare. This website gives access to well qualified and experienced baby sitters and teachers. Website allows to make bookings. Booking module was to be implemented in react-redux. That is what I did there in two months contractorship.

Now I have started looking for fulltime fullstack or frontend developer position in a company where the team environment is friendly and collaborative and open to new and innovative ideas.
